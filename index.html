Hobbies

Music

My father was a self-taught guitar player and was always playing music around the house. I initially got started in music playing the guitar when I was around 7 or 8 just like my dad, although I wasn't very good and I lost interest. However, in 5th grade I joined the school band and began to play the trumpet, which I had a natural aptitude for. I was the first chair trumpet player at my middle school and high school as well as having played with the Elgin Youth Symphony Orchestra. In addition to the trumpet, I am also a self-taught piano and bass player.

I generally prefer linears, but I also love a good tactile. My current daily driver is the NK65 Entry Edition with AKKO Neon keycaps and Glorious Panda switches.
